The Godfather

The Godfather is a critically acclaimed 1972 American epic crime film directed by Francis Ford Coppola, commonly regarded as one of the best and most powerful films ever made. Based on Mario Puzo's best-selling novel, the movie chronicles the Corleone crime family's saga in New York between 1945 and 1955. Key Informat
